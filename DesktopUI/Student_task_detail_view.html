Task Description

Please solve the following problems related to partial derivatives and multiple integrals. Show all your steps for full credit.

  1. Partial Differentiation

    Find the partial derivatives $f_x$ and $f_y$ for the function $f(x, y) = x^3 y^2 + \sin(xy)$.

  2. Double Integrals

    Evaluate the double integral $\iint_R (x + 2y) \, dA$ where $R$ is the region bounded by $y = x^2$ and $y = \sqrt{x}$.

  3. Gradient Vectors

    Find the gradient of $f(x, y, z) = e^{x-y} \cos(z)$ at the point $(1, 1, 0)$.
